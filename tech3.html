THE ENDURANCE CIRCUIT

Event Description

Embark on a thrilling voyage through the vast universe of electronics with The Endurance Circuit. This event pushes participants to blend fundamental concepts, hands-on circuit-building skills, and sharp troubleshooting abilities. Each round represents a critical phase of your mission — from powering up your systems to navigating deep-space challenges. Every precise calculation and flawless connection will determine whether your circuit endures the journey or falls apart in the void.

Round 1 - Ignition Grid

Solve a technical crossword grid to demonstrate your foundational knowledge. This represents the ignition phase of a rocket launch.

Round 2 - Data from Miller's Planet

Face circuit flash challenges and quick problem-solving tasks. Test your speed, precision, and clarity of thought to keep your spacecraft operational.

Round 3 - Docking Maneuver

Perform detailed circuit analysis, identify components, and fix issues like incorrect polarity or missing connections. Prove your skills as a mission engineer and return victorious.
