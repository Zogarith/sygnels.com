Description

Step aboard a cinematic voyage with Interstellar Cine-Quest, the ultimate movie-themed challenge for film enthusiasts! This event combines wit, speed, and keen observation as you navigate through galaxies of hidden clues and rapid-fire questions. From deciphering iconic scenes to racing against time, each round will test your cinematic knowledge and reflexes. Whether you’re a casual viewer or a true film explorer, this quest is your ticket to movie glory across the stars!

Round 1

Solve visual puzzles and decode movie titles using clever connections, pictorial hints, and iconic stills. Only keen observation and quick thinking will lead to success.

Round 2

A fast-paced cine rapid-fire quiz covering everything from legendary dialogues to blockbuster trivia. Speed and presence of mind are key to winning!
