TESSERACT PROMPTS

Event Description

Embark on a Tesseract puzzle quest where your logic, creativity, and problem-solving skills are your greatest assets. This one-of-a-kind event blends analytical thinking with modern technology, taking you on a journey from cracking intricate puzzles to unleashing AI-driven creativity. Participants will first tackle challenging puzzles to reveal hidden words and then transform those words into stunning AI-generated images. It’s the ultimate fusion of brainpower and tech artistry, where every solution sparks a new creation!

Round 1 - Decoding the Bulk

Each team will be given three sets of questions. Solving each set reveals a word by connecting the first letters of the answers. By the end, the team will have three words.

Round 2 - Imagining a Habitable Zone

Using the three words, teams must create a single creative prompt to generate an AI-based image.
