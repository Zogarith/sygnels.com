About Us

College of Alagappa Chettiar

Alagappa Chettiar Government College of Engineering and Technology (ACGCET), Karaikudi, established in 1952, stands as a premier institution dedicated to excellence in technical education, innovation, and research. With a strong legacy of producing skilled engineers and leaders, the college fosters an environment that blends academic rigor with practical learning. Known for its vibrant campus, state-of-the-art facilities, and committed faculty, ACGCET continues to empower students with knowledge, values, and creativity

About the Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, established in 1968, offers B.E. (ECE) and M.E. (Microwave & Optical Communication) programs. Equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and guided by experienced faculty, the department provides strong practical and academic training, fostering excellence in areas such as Telecommunication, Signal Processing, RF Circuits, VLSI Design, and Optical Communication. Recognized as a Collaborative Research Centre by Anna University, Chennai, the department is active in cutting-edge research and boasts a consistent record of excellent placements in reputed organizations. Committed to innovation, research, and industry readiness, it continues to shape competent engineers for the future.
