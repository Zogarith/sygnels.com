COSMIC LOGIC

Event Description

Blast off into the world of digital design with Cosmic Logic, an exciting event for aspiring circuit designers. Test your skills in Boolean algebra, logic gates, K-maps, and flip-flops as you design and implement combinational and sequential circuits. Turn theory into practice and showcase your problem-solving abilities in real-world digital design challenges.

Round 1 - Escape Velocity

Test your fundamentals of digital electronics through Boolean algebra, logic gates, flip-flops, and basic circuit design. Strong logic is your fuel to achieve escape velocity!

Round 2 - Orbital Maneuvers

Stabilize your spacecraft's orbit by mastering sequential circuits. Counters, shift registers, and design-based problems will test how well you control the flow of bits through time.
