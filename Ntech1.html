The Lazarus Mission

Description

An action-packed and thrilling event that tests memory, teamwork, and strategy, The Lazarus Mission takes participants on an adventurous journey through two challenging rounds. Combining sharp observation with a high-stakes race for survival, this event promises both excitement and intense competition as teams fight to complete their mission and return victorious.

Round 1

Planetary Reconnaissance

Participants will be shown an image containing multiple objects. They'll get time to observe and memorize it. Questions will then be asked about the image's details.

Round 2

Relativistic Race

10 teams compete to reach a finishing line set at 30 steps. Each team rolls a dice to determine how many steps they can move forward. A correct answer moves them forward the full dice value, while a wrong answer moves them back 1 space as a penalty. The first team to reach the finish line wins!.
