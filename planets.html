INSTRUCTIONS

  • Individual registration is required for all participants.
  • Team members should use the same team name.

  • Winners will be decided by judges appointed by the committee.
  • Decisions are final and binding.
  • Food and snacks will be provided 🍴.
  • E-certificates will be awarded to all participants.
  • Participants should be present at the venue on time as per the schedule.
  • For any clarification or queries, please contact the organizers.
