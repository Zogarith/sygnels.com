WORKSHOP

PCB DESIGNING

  • Gain hands-on experience in transforming circuit ideas into fabrication ready circuits.
  • Learn the fundamentals of PCB design including schematic creation and component placement.
  • Understand routing techniques and best practices using industry-standard design software.
  • Explore design rules, simulation, and fabrication processes to make boards production-ready.
  • Get step-by-step knowledge of the workflow from concept to manufacturing.
  • By the end, you’ll be able to: Design and develop your own PCB from scratch.

PRE-REQUIREMENTS

All participants are requested to pre-install KiCad 9.0 which is available at https://www.kicad.org/ in your laptops prior to the workshop.

You should understand the basics of series and parallel connections, biasing, and power supply fundamentals.

You should be able to interpret and analyze simple circuit diagrams.

You should have a clear understanding of how to test and verify a circuit virtually using simulation tools.
